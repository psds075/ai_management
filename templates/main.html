Who We Are?

We are developing algorithms for automatic diagnosis of dental imaging images such as automatic panoramic image diagnosis, automatic CBCT image diagnosis, and automatic cephalo image diagnosis.

  • 연동 가능 기기 VATEC, RAY, GENORAY, HDXWILL 등
